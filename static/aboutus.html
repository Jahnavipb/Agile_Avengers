About Us

Solaris T6 is a leading provider of solar energy solutions for homes and businesses. Our mission is to help our customers save money on their energy bills and reduce their carbon footprint.

We believe that solar energy is the future of energy and we are committed to making it accessible to everyone. That's why we offer a range of affordable solar products and services to meet the needs of our customers.

Our team consists of experienced solar energy experts who are passionate about renewable energy and committed to providing the highest quality products and services to our customers. We take pride in our work and strive to exceed our customers' expectations.

At Solaris T6, we are dedicated to helping our customers achieve their energy goals and making the world a better place for future generations.
